About PICT

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is one of the leading engineering colleges in India, located in Pune, Maharashtra. Established in 1983, PICT has been at the forefront of technical education.

Vision

To be a premier institute delivering quality technical education and research.

Mission

To produce competent engineers with a passion for learning and innovation.
